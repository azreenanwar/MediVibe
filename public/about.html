About MediVibe

MediVibe is designed to support medication routines with a calm interface, simple structure, and clear tracking.

Why it matters

Medication adherence can be difficult when schedules are busy or instructions are complex. A consistent routine helps improve outcomes and reduces missed doses.

What MediVibe provides

  • Structured medication schedule by dose and time
  • Reminder prompts designed for clarity
  • Adherence history to review progress
  • Secure sign-in for organized access

Privacy

This interface is a prototype. In a production version, data protection controls and secure storage would be implemented.
